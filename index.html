94Fifty Bluetooth Basketball

“The way that technology is changing pretty much everything we do, it's no surprise that someone created this Bluetooth basketball adds a technological dimension to basketball coaching. The ball then communicates with a smartphone app available on iOS or Android that shares all the biometric readings from the ball. The SwingSmart Golf Swing sensor

“The SwingSmart system is a golf swing sensor combined with an app that captures and analyzes every part of your swing. Once you've connected the sensor and downloaded the app you're able to see your tempo, swing speed, face angle, angle of attack and club shaft lean. The app even gives you a 3D visual of your swing from any angle. SwingSmart saves every swing you record and labels them "best" to "worst" so you can see what you're doing right and where you can improve.”

what is a basketball?
A basketball is a round, inflated ball used in the sport of basketball. It is made of synthetic rubber, leather, or composite material, and is typically orange in color. The ball has a circumference of approximately 29.5 inches, and weighs around 20 ounces. It is designed to be bounced and thrown, and is used in both indoor and outdoor games. The ball has a high degree of bounce, allowing players to perform a variety of moves and tricks during play. In addition to its use in the sport of basketball, the ball is also commonly used for other recreational purposes, such as in playground games and pickup games.
